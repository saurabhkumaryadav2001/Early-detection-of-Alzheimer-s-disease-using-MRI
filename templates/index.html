Curious Minds Want to Know

What causes Alzheimer's?

Alzheimer's is a complex disease with various contributing factors, including genetics and lifestyle choices.

How can I help a loved one with Alzheimer's?

Support and understanding are key. Engage in activities they enjoy and create a safe environment.

Is there a cure for Alzheimer's?

Currently, there is no cure, but ongoing research offers hope for future treatments.

Can Alzheimer's be prevented?

While prevention strategies are not definitive, maintaining a healthy lifestyle may reduce the risk.

How does Alzheimer's progress over time?

Alzheimer's typically advances in stages, affecting memory, cognition, and daily functioning.
